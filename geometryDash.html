Geometry Dash

This game is very simple, that can be played on all devices. It is a one button game, where you are a cube that automatically goes to the right of the screen while you jump over spikes. There are other modes, like the ship, the ball, and the wave that all have different abilites.

Creativity

Since everyone and anyone can make their own levels, these levels can defy the laws of the game. These can make for cinematics and great levels.

Base Game

The first level of the game, not featuring custom user-made levels, starts off with a cube that you control. The cube starts itself, so you will have to click a button to make it jump over spikes and blocks. Further in the level, you are introduced to the ship gamemode, which lets you rocket up when you touch the screen. The more levels you play, the more mechanics that are introduced.
